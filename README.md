# AI in the Screen Sector: Casebook
In June 2025, the CoSTAR Foresight Lab published *AI in the Screen Sector: Perspectives and Paths Forward* – the first UK-wide exploration of the overall impact of generative artificial intelligence (AI) on the full breadth of the UK’s screen sector.

This repository has been created to collect case studies of generative AI applications across film, television and video games. Review the case studies in the case-studies/posts folder, or view the published versions at [brtarran.github.io/ai-case-studies](https://brtarran.github.io/ai-case-studies/).

Interested in submitting a case study? [Send us an email](mailto:rsu-enquiries@bfi.org.uk).
